Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients: 2 eggs, 6tbsps of milk, a chunk of butter and a pinch of salt.
Equipment: a bowl, a whisk, a non-stick frying pan and a wooden spoon.
Instructions:
1) crack 2 eggs into a bowl and add the milk
2) whisk until the mix looks consistent
3) heat the frying pan up and add some butter
4) add the mix, let it settle and add the salt
5) stir with spoon until it looks good
6) et voila
